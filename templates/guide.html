User Guide

  1. Create an account from the Register page.
  2. Login using your email and password.
  3. On the Home page, type a single feedback sentence and click “Analyze Sentence”.
  4. To analyze many feedback entries, upload a TXT or CSV file and click the corresponding button.
  5. Check “History” to view all previous analyses for your account.
